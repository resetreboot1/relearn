Water Spinach

Description

Water spinach is a delicious food that I love. Why am I mentioning my personal taste? Well, because I do not know what I should write.

Ingredients

  • Water spinach
  • Oil
  • Garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Make sure the water spinach is completely dry.
  2. Coat the water spinach in oil.
  3. Add garlic cloves.
  4. Put it in the air fryer and then serve.
