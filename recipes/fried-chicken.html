Fried Chicken

Description

Fried Chicken is a delicious food that I love. Why am I mentioning my personal taste? Well, because I do not know what I should write.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Breading
  • Oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Marinate the Chicken for some time in the fridge.
  2. Take the Chicken out and use breading to coat it.
  3. Put some oil in a frying pan and heat it up slightly.
  4. Fry the chicken in the oil in batches and serve.
